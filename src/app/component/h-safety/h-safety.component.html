WHEN MEETING SOMEONE FOR THE FIRST TIME, PLEASE REMEMBER

It's very important to be safe when you meet people when exchanging money and book(s).

  • Insist on a public meeting place like a cafe surrounded by people
  • Insist on meeting at campus daylight
  • Travel there on your own – don’t accept a lift from the other person
  • Do tell a friend or family member who you are meeting, where you are going and when you will be back
  • Take your cell phone with you at all times
  • Consider having a friend accompany you
  • Trust your instincts
